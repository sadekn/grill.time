Our Story

Lebanon's flavor, lit by charcoal.

Grill Time is a premium Lebanese grill born inside Tawfeer Supermarket Kfar Aqqa. Every wrap, plate, and box is built around real charcoal fire, fresh-prepped meats, and the signature sauces of Time Food — the Lebanese sauce house behind our garlic, hummus, and tarator.

  • 🔥 100% Charcoal Grilled
  • 🥩 Fresh-Prepped Daily
  • 🧄 Time Food Sauces
  • 🌿 Lebanese Recipes
